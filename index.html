About Me

As for technical skills, I possess proficiency in programming languages such as C, C++, Python, and Java. Overall, I am driven to continuously expand my knowledge and skills in the fields of web development, competitive programming, and cybersecurity.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively seek opportunities to broaden my knowledge and skills in the field of computer engineering. I am an avid learner, always keeping myself updated with the latest advancements and trends. I frequently engage in online courses, tutorials, and workshops to further enhance my expertise.
